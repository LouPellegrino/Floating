Things to consider

Floating device
As it will be a little chilly tomorrow. The kmart boats are ideal as you dont have to be immersed in water. If room in your car allows - I would recommend half inflating your floating devices before you leave your homes, it always takes a longer time than expected (think mattress pumps if you have one).

Float times and river flow
Times are estimated. The float can take anywhere from 1hr - 3hrs. It depends on the water level, it is a total distance of 4km. Some areas are faster, some are slower. It has rained alot in Warrandtye the last 24hrs (Friday 15/01) which means the water will be flowing quite quickly. Which means it will be quicker and a little more adventurous. There are some mini rapids about 3 in one area that are usually ankle deep. When there is more water the rapids are a little faster.

Food/Snacks and or Drinks
Feel free to bring drinks alcoholic or not (don't bring glass).Just over halfway there is an area we can pull up and take a break and eat some snacks. So bring along something to munch on. Just be wary to keep it in something that is waterproof - so either presealed snacks ie wrapped packets or in ziplocks in a plastic bag etc. If you have dry bags even better.
Bring water - if you are someone that has to have water on them 24/7 like Kat and I, bring a plastic bottle that can seal and float.

Other things to consider

Weather and Clothing
The weather will be a little chilly, so bring a towel and warm layers for afterwards. Wear what ever you feel comfortable with in the water. I'll be wearing bathers, you do you - if you have a wet suit and want to wear that go for it.

Shoes
If you have water shoes or sandals with straps they are ideal for the short 300m walk back to the car. However, not necesary. If you have the boat you could also throw your thongs in there.

Rope
If you have some rope around the house bring some (again, not necessary - we will bring some extra - it just makes it easier to stay connected for periods of time).

Speaker
If you have a ueboom speaker we could link multiple together.

Phones
Take them or leave them, up to you. I usually take mine and put it in a dry bad and or double ziplock bags.
